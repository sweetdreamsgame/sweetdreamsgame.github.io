Description

Sweet Dreams is a pillow fight party game. Primarily it is an multiplayer local game.

History

How it began

Sweet Dreams development began in 2017 during a game jam. Two indie game studios, Heads or Tails and Oniric Pixel, got together to win this competition. And guess what? Sweet Dreams got the 1st place.

Keep moving

After winning the first phase of Epic Game Jam, the game was shown in several game events. Its public began to grow, bringing a lot of food for new ideas. New features coming from players' feedback were incorporated.
