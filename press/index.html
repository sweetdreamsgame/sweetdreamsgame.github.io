Description

Sweet Dreams is a pillow fighting game in local multiplayer arena, where your only goal is to put your friends to sleep.

Genres: Party Game, Fighting Game

Storyline

Sandman, the god of dreams, has summoned the most skilled fighters to batlle in the greatest pillow fight tournament ever. Billy Dream and his friends got into it and now they have to fight against their worst nightmares to defeat the great champion and have Sweet Dreams again.

Our History

Sweet Dreams development began in April of 2017 during the Epic Game Jam. Two indie game studios, Heads or Tails Games and Oniric Pixel, got together and formed Oniric Tails. Our game, Sweet Dreams, got the 1st place and went to the big final at Rock in Rio 2017. We got 3rd place in the technical jury and in the fan's choice.

Now we continue to develop the game to fulfill our game dev studio dream of living from our own production.
